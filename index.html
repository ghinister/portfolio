Cave Delver

Cave Delver is a Metroidvania game where the player take a role as an Explorer who delve into the deep cave to reveal the mysterious of the world. Gathering resources. Collect the lost artifact and fight the monsters.

I take a part as a Project Manager, Programmer, and Lead Game Designer. This project was started as I decided to take a part in NSC (National Software Contest) in early of 2023. Most of the time I working on the game system such as Player's control, Inventory System, Enemy's AI using state machine, Research System where player can give material to the researcher and acquire new blueprint for more tools. The project didn't make it to the final round but it's still a good experience so far.
